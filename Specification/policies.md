# Internal Messaging Community Policies
**Online Community Platform for Tabletop Miniature Hobbyists**

**Effective Date:** 21 April 2026  
**Applies To:** All users, moderators, and administrators using the platform’s internal messaging features (DMs, group chats, system comments).

---

## 1. Purpose & Scope
These policies define acceptable use of internal messaging to ensure respectful, focused, and lawful communication. They apply to all private messages, group messages, and any platform-supported direct communication.

Internal messaging is provided to support collaboration, hobby discussion, moderation actions, and community-building—not as a general-purpose social network or marketplace.

---

## 2. Core Principles
All internal communications must adhere to these principles:
- **Respect:** Treat all members with courtesy and professionalism.
- **Relevance:** Keep messages aligned with hobby-related or platform-related purposes.
- **Safety:** Protect personal data and emotional well-being.
- **Legality:** Respect intellectual property and applicable laws.
- **Good Faith:** Engage honestly and avoid deceptive or manipulative behavior.

---

## 3. General Rules for Internal Communication
- Be clear, constructive, and civil.
- Use language appropriate for a diverse, international hobby community.
- Follow moderator instructions when provided through internal messages.
- Do not attempt to bypass platform rules by moving prohibited behavior into private messages.
- The platform reserves the right to review internal messages when required for moderation, safety, or legal compliance.

---

## 4. Harassment, Abuse, and Hate Prohibited
Internal messaging must **not** be used for:
- Harassment, intimidation, bullying, or threats.
- Insults or derogatory remarks based on personal characteristics, skills, or preferences.
- Persistent unwanted messages after a recipient has asked you to stop.
- Encouraging self-harm, violence, or exclusion of others.

**Zero tolerance:** Severe or repeated harassment may result in immediate suspension or permanent ban.

---

## 5. Off-Topic and Misuse of Messaging
Internal messaging should not be misused for:
- Prolonged non-hobby or non-platform-related conversations.
- Political, religious, or real‑world ideological debates unrelated to tabletop miniature hobbies.
- Meme flooding, chain messages, or low‑effort noise.
- Coordinating rule violations on public areas of the platform.

Moderators may ask users to move casual chat to designated off-topic spaces or end it entirely.

---

## 6. Commercial Spam and Unsolicited Advertising
The following are **strictly forbidden** in internal messages without prior written permission from the platform:
- Unsolicited advertisements for products, services, or crowdfunding.
- Affiliate links, referral codes, or discount schemes.
- Mass messaging users to promote sales, commissions, or external shops.
- Repeated “soft selling” disguised as casual conversation.

Violation may lead to immediate restrictions on messaging privileges.

---

## 7. Self‑Promotion Limits
Limited self-promotion is allowed **only when relevant and consensual**:
- Sharing your work (e.g., custom sculpts, painted minis) **upon explicit request**.
- Responding to a direct inquiry about your services or creations.
- Coordinating allowed collaborations already approved by moderators.

Not allowed:
- Cold‑messaging users to showcase your work.
- Repeatedly promoting the same project or studio across multiple conversations.
- Using messaging as a substitute storefront or portfolio spam.

---

## 8. Doxxing and Privacy Violations
Absolutely prohibited:
- Sharing another person’s real name, address, email, phone number, workplace, or social accounts without explicit consent.
- Threatening to expose personal information.
- Collecting personal data via deception or pressure.
- Encouraging others to contact, harass, or investigate a user offline.

This rule applies **even if the information is publicly available elsewhere**.

---

## 9. Intellectual Property (IP) & Custom Sculpt Disputes
Given the nature of miniature hobby creation, special care is required.

### 9.1 Prohibited in Messages
- Accusations of theft, plagiarism, or IP infringement presented as harassment or public shaming.
- Demands, threats, or legal claims sent directly to another user.
- Sharing private commissions, files, or sculpt assets without permission.
- Pressuring others to admit infringement or concede ownership.

### 9.2 Proper Handling of IP Concerns
If you believe an IP violation has occurred:
1. **Do not** confront or harass the other party via messaging.
2. Collect objective evidence (links, timestamps, files).
3. Submit a formal report through the platform’s moderation or IP dispute channel.
4. Allow moderators to facilitate communication if needed.

### 9.3 Platform Stance
- Moderators do not provide legal advice.
- The platform may request temporary removal of disputed content while reviewing evidence.
- Final legal determinations remain the responsibility of the involved parties under applicable law.

---

## 10. Impersonation and Misrepresentation
Users must not:
- Impersonate moderators, administrators, or other community members.
- Claim official endorsement or authority they do not have.
- Misrepresent ownership of sculpts, designs, or studios.

---

## 11. Reporting & Moderation
- Users are encouraged to report violations using built‑in reporting tools.
- Reports are confidential to the extent possible.
- Retaliation against users who report violations is forbidden.
- Moderators may issue warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans depending on severity and history.

---

## 12. Enforcement Actions
Possible actions include:
- Informal warning or guidance.
- Temporary mute or messaging suspension.
- Account suspension or permanent removal.
- Content removal or access limitation.
- Escalation to legal authorities where required.

Enforcement decisions are final but may be appealed through the official appeal process.

---

## 13. Policy Updates
These policies may be updated as the community evolves. Continued use of internal messaging after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised rules.

---

## 14. Closing
Internal messaging is a privilege designed to enrich collaboration and creativity within the tabletop miniature hobby. Respectful, relevant, and lawful use helps keep the community welcoming and sustainable for everyone.
